      Installation:

      Download the Kernel and latest Magisk NetHunter Module and flash it
      Reboot
      Download chroot archive of your choice
      Extract the archive in /sdcard
      Open net hunter app
      Click on Kali chroot manager and select SD card
      Chroot will start installing (approx. 10 mins)
      Open net hunter terminal app
      Choose kali
      And type apt-get update && dist-upgrade
